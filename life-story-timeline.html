The Break-It Era

The Search Era
Moved cities. Joined a band. Fully convinced I was going to be a global drummer. Later pivoted to civil engineering and got the degree. Read widely. Showed up fully. Somehow kept ending up as a leader in almost every group I was part of.
theme: identity gets forged in search
ages 24-29
The Pivot Era
Tried to build a career in civil engineering. It didn't work out. Stumbled into tech and became a data engineer. Got married. Moved to the UK. Started a new life in a quiet city.
lesson: pivots create new futures
ages 29-36
The Dad Era
Best era so far. Had a daughter. Now I know every nursery rhyme by heart. Started my YouTube channel and newsletter. Started vibe coding and now pushing more PRs than Coca-Cola sells cans.
focus: family, craft, compounding
